Welcome to Westeros

Introduction

We are a group of dedicated LARPers who enjoy immersing ourselves in the world of George R.R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire series.

We aim to bring a high quality LARPING experience to the Calgary area, where nerds of all flavors(But especially A Song of Ice and Fire nerds!) can join us to eat, drink, dance and fight in a mythical land of heroism and tragedy.

Our events are designed to be as true to the books as possible, and we take pride in the quality of our costuming and props.

If you're interested in learning more about us, we invite you to watch the following videos. Our Contact Form is at the bottom of this page.
