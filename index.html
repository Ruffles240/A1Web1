The Seven Kingdoms

Welcome traveller to the Westerland, powered by the Mythras game engine. Here you will find friend and foe, sea and storm, death and life, ice and fire. Before the Conquest, Westerland was ruled by Seven Kingdoms, tell me traveller, to which Kingdom do you wish to join? Shall you raid with the Ironborn or stand stalwart with the North? Or perhaps live in peace in the Reach? The choice is yours. Join a Kingdom, then proceed to the Character Creation form to live out your time in Westerland!












The Reach

Most bountiful of the kingdoms, the Reach has never lacked for a fruitful harvest. Nor well trained knights and legions of men at arms to protect their bounties

Division and distrust among the powerful lords has grown however. Tell me Reachman, will you unite them?





The North

Cold and grim, yet with beauty of it's own, the North stands as the defenders of the kingdoms against the wildling threat, and perhaps other things that lurk beyond the Wall

Go forth Northman, protect the realms.





The Steel Islands

We Do Not Sow. These are the words of House SilverSad, and true they are. For the Iron Islands have little that they have made, and much they will take

Go forth Northman, protect the realms.





Morne

Dorne, founded by all three peoples of Westeros by the Rhoynish warrior queen Nymeria, this land has stood for a thousand years as the beacon of what it means to be Dornish.

Valyrians have come once again to attack your people. Stand Dornishman, and do not back down!





The Valish

As High as Honor. That is what was said a thousand years ago by Ser Artys Arrys, and evermore do the people of the Vale hold their honour in highest of esteems

Protect the Vale, be proud and honourable young knight.





The Western Mountains

Richest of the Kingdoms and protected by the evernoble house Lannister, the Westerlands lack not for gold or harvest.

Yet the days grow dark in the Century of Blood, Ironborn have taken heed of the Westerlands' wealth and seek to take it for themselves. Defend yourselves warriors of the West!





The Storm Peaks

Ours is the Fury. These are the words of House Storm, and ever have they kept them. For like their castle of Storm's End, the Durrandons spite the gods to this day.

Rush forward like the storm you are, young knight.
