History of the Seven Kingdoms

The ICE and FIRE LARP group was founded in Calgary, Alberta in the mid-2000s by a small group of friends who shared a passion for the epic fantasy world of "A Song of Ice and Fire" by George R.R. Martin. The group started small, but quickly grew in popularity among fantasy enthusiasts in the city.

At the beginning, ICE and FIRE LARP events were focused solely on medieval-style gameplay, with members taking on the roles of knights, lords, and ladies in a complex web of political intrigue and power struggles. But as the group grew, it began to incorporate more elements from the books, including fantastical creatures like dragons and direwolves.

One of the things that set ICE and FIRE LARP apart from other LARP groups in the area was their attention to detail when it came to costuming and props. Members spent countless hours creating intricate costumes, weapons, and set pieces to make the game world as immersive as possible.

Over the years, the group continued to evolve and grow, taking on new members and expanding its repertoire of events and gameplay styles. Today, ICE and FIRE LARP is a thriving community of fantasy enthusiasts who come together to tell epic stories, forge lasting friendships, and immerse themselves in the world of Westeros and beyond.
